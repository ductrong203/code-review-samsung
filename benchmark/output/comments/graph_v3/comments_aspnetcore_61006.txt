<note>**[Code Defect]** The S_addr field in SOCKADDR_IN is a union. Accessing it directly via S_un.S_addr is often incorrect depending on the CsWin32 generated structure layout, as it may require accessing the byte array or a specific union member. Using the raw field directly can lead to incorrect IP address parsing.

**[Performance]** The constructor for SocketAddressIPv4 copies the entire SOCKADDR_IN struct by value. Since this is a small fixed-size struct, this is acceptable, but the subsequent call to new IPAddress(_sockaddr.sin_addr.S_un.S_addr) creates a new object on every access. If GetIPAddress is called frequently, this causes unnecessary heap allocations.</note>
