However, table_open() can trigger cache invalidations that clear rd_amcache. Accessing the result of RelationGetHypercoreInfo after table_open() is a use-after-free/dangling pointer risk. The OID must be extracted before calling table_open().

**[Security Vulnerability]** The function hypercore_open_compressed calls RelationGetHypercoreInfo(relation) and immediately uses the result to call table_open. As noted in the function's own comment, table_open can trigger cache invalidations that free the HypercoreInfo structure. This creates a Use-After-Free (UAF) vulnerability where the pointer returned by RelationGetHypercoreInfo becomes dangling before it is dereferenced by the table_open call.</note>
