<note>**[Code Defect]** The logic for filtering nested modules is flawed because it uses a HashSet of PSModuleInfo objects. PSModuleInfo does not override Equals/GetHashCode based on identity or unique path, meaning the HashSet.Contains check will likely fail to identify the nested modules correctly, causing the filter to be ineffective.

**[Maintainability and Readability]** The logic for filtering nested modules is mixed directly into the main loop of CompleteModuleName, increasing the cognitive load of an already complex function. This should be extracted into a private helper method or a predicate to improve readability and maintainability.</note>
