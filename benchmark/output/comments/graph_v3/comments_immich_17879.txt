<note>**[Code Defect]** The jobId construction uses `item.data.id` and `item.data.recipientId`. If `item.data.recipientId` is undefined or null, the jobId becomes 'id/undefined', which will cause inconsistent job identification and potential failures in job removal or deduplication.

**[Security Vulnerability]** The jobId is constructed using string concatenation of user-controlled input (item.data.id and item.data.recipientId). If these inputs are not properly sanitized, this could lead to unexpected behavior in BullMQ job management, such as ID collisions or path-traversal-like issues if the job ID is used in filesystem-based storage backends.</note>
