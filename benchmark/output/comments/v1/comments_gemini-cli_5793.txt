<note>**[Code Defect]** The removal of `await config.initialize()` in `runNonInteractive` is a potential bug. 

The `Config` class now enforces a single-initialization check (added in `packages/core/src/config/config.ts`), and `loadCliConfig` (which calls `new Config(...)`) does not appear to call `initialize()` internally. If `runNonInteractive` is the entry point for non-interactive flows, the configuration might remain uninitialized, potentially causing `getFileService()` or other lazy-loaded dependencies to fail or behave unexpectedly when accessed later. Ensure that `initialize()` is called exactly once in the lifecycle of the `Config` object.</note>
