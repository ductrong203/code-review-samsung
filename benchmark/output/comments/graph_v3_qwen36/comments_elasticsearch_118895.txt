**[Security Vulnerability]** The `create` method in `QueryMaxAnalyzedOffset` allows a negative value (-1) to default to the index's `maxAnalyzedOffset`. If the index setting is misconfigured or too high, this could lead to resource exhaustion (DoS) by processing excessively large fields. This is a potential DoS vector.</note>
