**[Maintainability and Readability]** The GetDevices method has become a gatekeeper for Bluetooth initialization, mixing high-level business logic (deciding whether to fetch devices) with low-level Bluetooth adapter lifecycle management. This violates the Single Responsibility Principle.</note>
